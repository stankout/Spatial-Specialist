# Brand System

The visual direction is premium, architectural, editorial, and calm—not decorative luxury.

- Charcoal `#171a17`: authority and technical clarity.
- Warm ivory `#f3f0e8`: editorial background.
- Deep forest `#203b32`: spatial consultation and calm.
- Muted brass `#b4894d`: restrained emphasis, not ornamental gold.
- Georgia/system serif: editorial headings with broad Vietnamese glyph support.
- Arial/system sans: readable interface and body copy.

Use generous space, strong hierarchy, thin rules, and restrained motion. Avoid purple AI gradients, icon spam, fake stock portraits, unnecessary carousels, and copied third-party layouts.
