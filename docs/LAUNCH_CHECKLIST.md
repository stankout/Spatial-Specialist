# Launch Checklist

## Owner information required

- Approved Anh Cao portrait and company logo
- Public phone, email, canonical domain, and booking URL
- Brokerage name/URL and Georgia real estate license details, if active
- Verified InterNACHI membership, CPI status, insurance, and certification details
- Approved social profile URLs
- Confirmed service areas, offered services, pricing approach, and scheduling rules
- Genuine reviews with source, date, URL, consent, and verification status
- Approved English/Vietnamese biography
- Real videos, thumbnails, transcripts, and a redacted sample report

## Technical

- Connect lead delivery and spam protection
- Add analytics IDs only after consent/privacy decisions
- Replace `https://example.com` through `NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_URL`
- Review privacy policy and terms with qualified counsel
- Test keyboard, screen reader basics, phone, tablet, and desktop
- Verify all credentials and hide unavailable services
- Run lint, typecheck, test, and production build
- Review Vercel Preview before production promotion
